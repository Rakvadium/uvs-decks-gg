## Life Tracker Enhancements

### 1. Voice Control

"Hey Universus, subtract 5 from rival" - hands-free health adjustment using voice commands.

### 2. Gesture-Based Health Changes

Swipe gestures for quick adjustments: swipe up +5, swipe down -5, circular motion for +10/-10.

### 3. Calculator Mode

Tap the health number to open a calculator for complex damage: "20 - 7 + 3 - 2 = 14".

### 4. Health Presets

Quick buttons for common damage values based on the current meta (e.g., -5, -6, -7 for common attack damages).

### 5. Shake to Reset

Physically shake the phone to trigger the reset/new game confirmation dialog.

### 6. Double-Tap Health for Max

Double-tap health number to instantly set to maximum (useful after effects that set health to starting value).

### 7. Health Milestones

Visual indicators when health crosses key thresholds (50%, 25%, 10%, "Danger Zone").

### 8. Momentum Counter

Built-in momentum tracking that auto-increments based on attack resolution.

### 9. Timer Integration

Built-in round timer with customizable durations and audio alerts.

### 10. Turn Counter

Track which turn number the game is on, useful for effects that trigger on specific turns.

### 11. First Player Indicator

Coin flip animation to determine first player, with indicator showing who went first.

### 12. Card Pool Counter

Track how many cards are in each player's card pool (discard pile equivalent).

### 13. Deck Size Tracker

Show remaining cards in deck based on starting deck size and cards drawn/milled.

### 14. Hand Size Display

Track current hand size for both players with visual indicators.

### 15. Stamina Tracking

Dedicated stamina counter that reflects character's stamina stat.

### 16. Multi-Character Support

Support for tag-team formats where multiple characters share a health pool or have separate health.

### 17. Health Lock

Lock one player's health to prevent accidental changes during complex effect resolution.

### 18. Health History Scrubber

Timeline scrubber to visually navigate through health changes during the game.

### 19. Quick Notes

Attach quick notes to health changes explaining why they happened.

### 20. Floating Health Widget

Minimized floating widget showing health when switching to other apps (for rulebook reference).
